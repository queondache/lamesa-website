Frequently asked questions

Do I need previous experience?

No. The Weekly Studio and Drop-in Session are perfect for beginners who have never touched clay before. The Flex Studio and Wheel Coworking do require some prior experience.

How much do ceramics classes in Barcelona cost?

At La Mesa prices start from 20€/hour for Drop-in Sessions up to 120€/month for the Weekly Studio. Our Wheel Intro course is 90€.

Are materials included?

Yes. All our classes and workshops include tools, materials and firings.

Where are you in Barcelona?

We're at Carrer de l'Atlàntida 47, in the La Barceloneta neighbourhood. We're the only ceramics studio in La Barceloneta (a five-minute walk from the beach).

Can I book online?

Yes. The Weekly Studio and Drop-in Session can be booked directly online. For the Flex Studio, Wheel Intro and Wheel Coworking, message us on WhatsApp to arrange a time.

Do you do private group sessions or events?

Yes. We organise team buildings, birthdays, hen parties and family sessions for groups of up to 10 people. Message us for a personalised quote.

Are you an artist or creator?

Would you like to run a workshop at La Mesa? If you make something that can be shared around a table (for up to 10 people), we'd love to meet you.
