## Plans

### Taller Semanal Modelado
- Price: €120 per month.
- Includes: 4 weekly sessions of 2 hours, hand-building (modelado) techniques, finishing and glazing, all materials and firings.
- For: beginners welcome, no experience required.
- Booking: online via Stripe Checkout.

### Taller Semanal Torno
- Price: €160 per month.
- Includes: 4 weekly sessions of 2 hours, wheel throwing (torno), all materials and firings.
- For: beginners welcome, no experience required.
- Booking: online via Stripe Checkout.

### Clase Suelta Modelado
- Price: €50.
- Includes: one drop-in hand-building session, minimum 2 hours, all materials and firing. No monthly commitment.
- Availability: Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday.
- Booking: online via Stripe Checkout.

### Clase Suelta Torno
- Price: €70.
- Includes: one private wheel-throwing session, minimum 2 hours, maximum 2 people, all materials and firing. No monthly commitment.
- Availability: Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday.
- Booking: online via Stripe Checkout.

### Intro al Torno
- Price: €90.
- Includes: intensive 2-session course (2h each) on throwing and trimming, maximum 2 people.
- Booking: via WhatsApp.

### Coworking Torno
- Price: €20 per hour (minimum 2 hours).
- Includes: open wheel access for experienced potters who work autonomously.
- Availability: Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday, 14:00–18:00.
- Booking: via WhatsApp.

### Taller Flex
- Price: from €120 (8h). For more hours, contact via WhatsApp.
- For: students with prior experience, flexible hand-building or wheel hours.
- Booking: via WhatsApp (https://wa.me/34711552030).

### Creative workshops
- Price: variable per workshop, see website.
- Includes: themed sessions with guest artists; all materials.

### Private sessions
- Price: custom quote.

- Booking: via WhatsApp or email.
